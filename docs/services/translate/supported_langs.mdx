# Supported languages

<img src="/img/docs/langauges.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />

Welcome to the Heexy Translate article! Heexy Translate is a powerful translation service that provides fast and accurate language translation capabilities. In this article, you will learn about all the supported and unsupported languages that Heexy Translate is capable of translating.

Heexy Translate is a cutting-edge translation service designed to facilitate seamless communication across different languages. With its advanced algorithms and state-of-the-art technology, Heexy Translate delivers swift and precise translations, making it an indispensable tool for individuals and businesses alike.

## Supported and Unsupported Languages

In this article, you will discover the extensive list of languages supported by Heexy Translate. Whether you're looking to translate from English to Spanish or Chinese to French, Heexy Translate has got you covered. Additionally, we will also explore the languages that are currently not supported by Heexy Translate, providing you with a comprehensive overview of its capabilities.

Stay tuned to explore the world of language translation with Heexy Translate!
