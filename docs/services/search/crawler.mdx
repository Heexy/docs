# Heexy Bot (Crawler)

**Heexybot** is our official web crawler that visits websites and indexes their content for our search engine.

Thanks to Heexybot, we can provide **fast, accurate, and up-to-date search results** for our users.

---

### 🔍 What does Heexybot do?

- 📥 Collects and indexes **public web content**  
- 🚫 **Blocks malicious websites** and low-quality sources  
- 🧠 Focuses only on **relevant and important** content to reduce spam  
- 📡 Helps build and update Heexy's search index  
- 🔐 Respects website rules (`robots.txt`)  
- 🤝 Follows ethical crawling practices  

---

### 📌 Important Notes

- Heexybot does **not** track or collect personal data  
- Operates mainly from **European servers**  
- Clearly identifies itself with a custom **User-Agent**
