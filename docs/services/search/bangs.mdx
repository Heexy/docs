**Heexy !Bangs** are designed to **speed up your searching and browsing** experience.  
These handy shortcuts let you search directly on other websites or quickly redirect to them — saving you time and clicks.

---

### 🛠️ How to Use Heexy !Bangs?
There are two main ways to use Bangs, depending on your needs:
### 1. Redirect on website: `!bang_name`
    - 🚀 Example: `!wikipedia` → Redirects you to Wikipedia.
### 2. Search on website: `!bang_name search_term`
    - 🚀 Example: `!yt funny cat videos` → Searches YouTube for “funny cat videos”.
