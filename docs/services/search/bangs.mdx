**Heexy !Bangs** are designed to **speed up your searching and browsing** experience.  
!Bangs are shortcuts that let you search directly on other websites or quickly redirect to them — saving you a lot of time.

---

### 🛠️ How to Use Heexy !Bangs?
There are two main ways to use Bangs:
### 1. Redirect on website: `!bang_name`
    - 🚀 Example: `!wikipedia` → Redirects you to Wikipedia saving you a trip to the search engine results page.
### 2. Search on website: `!bang_name search_term`
    - 🚀 Example: `!yt 1 hour of plink (with sound)` → Searches YouTube for “[1 hour of plink (with sound)](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=1%20hour%20of%20plink%20(with%20sound))”
