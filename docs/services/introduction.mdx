# Introduction to Services

Heexy offers many services that you can use.

Heexy provides a range of services to cater to your needs. Explore our diverse services to enhance your online experience.

### 🔍 [Heexy Search Engine](https://heexy.org/)
Experience a fast, reliable, and privacy-focused search engine that delivers accurate search results without intrusive advertisements.

**Key Features:**
- 🛡️ Privacy-first approach  
- 🚫 Ad-free search experience  
- ⚡ Fast and accurate results  
- 🌍 Comprehensive web coverage

### 🗺️ [Heexy Maps](https://maps.heexy.org/)
Discover the world with our advanced mapping service. Explore interactive maps, get directions, and find points of interest.

**Key Features:**
- 🧭 Modern mapping technology  
- 🖱️ Interactive navigation  
- 📍 Points of interest discovery  
- 🛣️ Route planning and directions

### 🌎 [Heexy Translate](https://translate.heexy.org/)
Access powerful translation services supporting over 130 languages with API integration capabilities.

**Key Features:**
- 🌐 130+ supported languages  
- 💬 Free and unlimited translations  
- 🛠️ API access for developers  
- 🔊 Text-to-speech functionality
