# Languages supported

Here is information about the languages that are supported and are translated

The translation of our services is very important to us, we try to translate everything into as many languages as possible, and we try to find and work with family speakers and our community to make the translations as accurate as possible, we want everyone in the world to have access to Heexy in their native language, because Heexy is for the whole world.
