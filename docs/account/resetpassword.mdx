# Resseting passwords

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';
import ThemedImage from '@theme/ThemedImage';

<ThemedImage

1. Go to the [Heexy Account page](https://account.heexy.org/).
2. Click on the input field and **enter your email**.
3. Click on the **Continue** button.
4. Check your email for a **password reset link**.
   > Note: If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
5. Click the **one-time login link** that has been sent to your email.
6. Login to your account.
7. **Change your password** in the settings.

:::warning 
Never give your one-time login link to anyone, no matter how trustworthy they sound. \
Never reset passwords on public WiFi networks.
:::
