# How to reset password?

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

1. Go to the [Heexy Account](https://account.heexy.org/) page.
2. Click on the input field and enter your email.
3. Click on the **Continue** button.
4. Check your email for a password reset link.
   - Note: If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
5. Login in you account.
6. Click on the link in the email what will login you in.
7. Change your password in the settings.

:::warning Heexy will never ask for your password or any sensitive information always ensure you are on the official Heexy website before entering any personal information.
