# Account hacked or stolen?
If you believe your Heexy account has been hacked or stolen, please follow these steps:
1. **Reset your password**: Go to the [Heexy Account](https://account.heexy.org/) click on input field and enter your email, then click on the **Continue** button.
2. **Check your email**: Look for an email with link. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
3. **Secure your account**: After resetting your password, review your account settings and ensure no unauthorized changes have been made.
