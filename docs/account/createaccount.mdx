# How to create an account?

1. Go to the [Heexy Account](https://account.heexy.org/).
2. Write your email in the input field.
3. Click on the **Countinue** button.
4. Check your email for a verification link.
- Note: If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
5. Click on the link to verify your email.
6. Done! You can now use your Heexy account.
